## Task

Proceed to summarize the conversation that has taken
place up to this point. 

## Requirements

The following requirements must be met:

- The summary *MUST* include all important information
- The summary *MUST* conserve tokens 
- The summary *MUST* reduce the overall context window size
- The summary *MUST NOT* include duplicate information 

## Context

Your response will be used as the foundation for a new 
conversation. If the model supports system messages,
your response will become a system message in a new
conversation. In other words, the target audience 
is the assistant or model rather than the user.
