## Task

Summarize the conversation so far into a minimal, lossless message

## Requirements

Preserve:
- facts, decisions, constraints, preferences
- technical details needed to continue the task

Remove:
- redundancy, examples, pleasantries, reasoning trails

Rules:
- no new info
- no commentary
- concise, neutral tone
- optimized for minimal tokens

## Context

Your response will become a system message in a new conversation.
